Paris

This city is probably the one you heard of a lot of times in your life. Paris (the capital of france) is known home to the famous french monument the eiffel tower. Built in france from 1887 to 1889 this iron builing reaches to the sky with a height of 300 meters.

Some other building and attractions you can see at paris are, the Arc de Triomphe, Notre Dame, and the Palace of Versailles. For those who are interested in fashion you can visit a fashion show in Paris with its exclusive dresses and unique designs. When you're are strolling along in Paris you can expect mimes wandering around the streets. They are rather funny but silent street preformers who dress in black and white and amuse people around them.
